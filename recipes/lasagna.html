Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

This recipe is perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook beef with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbly.
