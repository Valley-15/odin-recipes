Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish made with aromatic spices and marinated chicken.

It’s rich, spicy, and loved all over the world.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Biryani spices
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Cook rice halfway.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Cook on low heat until done.
